Hi I am John Doe. I am 36 years old, and at this point in my life, I want to dedicate myself to public service. I was born and raised in Newton, Urgandale, where I was raised by two loving parents. During the early years of my life I participated in the Urgandale youth group and played soccer. In Newton High School, I was a member of the science club and was interested in chemistry. After high school I attended Ohio State University, where I studied biology and law.

I started my political career when I was twenty five and worked on the school board for Rapture county. I played a critical role in unionizing the school curriculum across Urgandale. After that, I served as a congressman in the third district. There I influenced healthy meals in school cafeterias and passed legislation to reduce food waste in grocery stores.

I took a break from politics after the birth of my first child. I was dedicated to be there for my child in the early and most important years of their life. Now I wish to bring that dedication back to the community and serve the country as a senator. I think I can really make a difference in the world by giving it my all in the Senate.
