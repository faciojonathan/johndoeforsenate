If you are eager to support John Doe and his efforts to be elected on to the Urgandale
state senate, we would be glad to have your help. In order to help with our campaign, there
are a few requirements. They include:

  • Must be over 18 years old
  • Must be registered to vote
  • Able to communicate and interact with a variety of different people

Once on the campaign, you could be performing a wide range of different tasks. These include
(but are not limited to):

  • Phone banking, where you will call different people and explain to them our candidates positions.
  • Block walking, where you go door to door for likely voters talk about our candidate.
  • Assisting at rallies. This may include clean up afterwards or setting up equipment.
  • Composing emails and scripts for our campaign.

If you believe you will be a helpful addition to our campaign, please contact Jack Henry at
555-456-7645 or at jack.henry@johndoe.org

John Doe's campaign is also welcome to financial assistance. If you would like to donate to our campaign contact our office for mailing information.
